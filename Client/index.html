Welcome!

The Nim Game it's a game of strategy where two players play by turns.
In each turn, the player has to take one or more pieces from one the stacks.
The game objective is to be the player to take the last piece of the board.
If you want to play, click on the "Play" button that is in the top of the page. Good luck!
If you want to check the ranking of the player's, you can click on the "Ranks" button and in case you have some question about the rules, you can see them by clicking in the "Rules" button.

Rules

1. The game countains one or more stakcs of pieces, with a determined number of pieces in them;
2. The moves are made by turns, and in one turn only one player can play, and the player can take one or more pieces from one stack;
3. In the end, the player who takes the last piece of the game, wins.
